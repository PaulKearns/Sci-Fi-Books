Why funny sci-fi?

I made this website because I read "The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy" and loved it, and want to help share and find other books like this.

Funny sci-fi combines speculative science fiction with humor, satire, or comedy. I enjoy their absurdist scenarios, witty dialogue, and social commentary while exploring interesting concepts. If you haven't read any before, give one of these a shot!
